Villager model assets
=====================

The GLB files in this directory are Quaternius models distributed under
Creative Commons Zero v1.0 Universal (CC0 1.0):
https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

Assets and source pages:

- quaternius-villager-man.glb
  "Man" by Quaternius, from the Animated Men Pack
  https://poly.pizza/m/fjHyMd5Wxw
  https://poly.pizza/bundle/Animated-Men-Pack-DAC9SDgMQT
  Downloaded GLB:
  https://static.poly.pizza/985eacbf-9dde-44b7-9270-4e35c8400b13.glb

- quaternius-villager-woman.glb
  "Woman in Dress" by Quaternius, from the Animated Women Pack
  https://poly.pizza/m/zMyPlQXBzq
  https://poly.pizza/bundle/Animated-Women-Pack-HHSKxnk1mY
  Downloaded GLB:
  https://static.poly.pizza/a642af96-e239-4c5f-b50d-7661ff51deec.glb

Both models retain their authored rigs and idle/walk animation clips. The game
scales them to settlement proportions and applies deterministic clothing, skin,
and hair colors at runtime. Attribution is not required by CC0, but this record
is kept with the assets so their provenance and reuse terms remain auditable.
