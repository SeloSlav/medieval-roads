Delivery cart model asset
=========================

quaternius-medieval-cart.glb is "Cart" by Quaternius, distributed under
Creative Commons Zero v1.0 Universal (CC0 1.0):
https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

Source model:
https://poly.pizza/m/l7bDe7ak6j

Collection:
https://poly.pizza/bundle/Medieval-Village-Pack-NsHhjhlrfY

Downloaded GLB:
https://static.poly.pizza/94b32c91-4d46-4172-aadd-10176a4eb543.glb

The game scales the cart, selects one of several deterministic canopy palettes,
and adds its existing cargo-specific load geometry at runtime. Attribution is
not required by CC0, but this record is kept with the asset for auditing.
